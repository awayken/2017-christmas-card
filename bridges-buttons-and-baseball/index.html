Bridges, Buttons and Baseball

You are brave. You march across the bridge with Mom and Kiddo. On the other side is a big door.

You don't know how to open the door. Kiddo finds a button. It is too high to push.

Kiddo likes to play baseball. Kiddo can throw a rock at the button. You find a rock. Kiddo throws it, and it pushes the button.

The door opens. You can see a cave. The cave has two paths. Do you go left, or do you go right?
